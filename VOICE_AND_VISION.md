## Language Rules

**Say this:**
- Stock up on your favorite art
- Speculative digital assets redeemable for more art
- Issued by artists
- Backed by gold
- Withdraw to Venmo instantly
- Direct connection to the artist
- Skin in the game
- Ancient wisdom and treasure
- A new layer on the internet — and you own it
- Protocol custody
- Training wheels that come off when they're ready
- Magical, mythical, full of possibility
- Reserve vault
- Stewardship to sovereignty

**Never say:**
- Investment
- Returns
- Stock market
- Platform (when referring to Zeyoda or Artistocks — this is a layer, not a platform)
- Expectation of profit
- Users (say fans, artists, community)
- Web3 (unless technically necessary)
- Protocol custody model (fine internally; don't lead with it publicly)
